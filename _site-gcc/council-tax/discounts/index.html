Council Tax

Council Tax discounts and exemptions

You may qualify for a reduction or full exemption from Council Tax. The type of discount depends on who lives in your property and your personal circumstances.

Apply for Single Person Discount Check all discounts
25% off for one adult
50% off for some disabilities
100% exempt if all students

Types of discount

Single Person Discount

25% off if you are the only adult living in your home as your main residence.

Find out more →

Student exemption

Properties occupied only by full-time students are fully exempt from Council Tax.

Find out more →

Disabled person reduction

If your home has been adapted for a disabled resident, your bill may be reduced.

Find out more →

Severe mental impairment

People with a severe mental impairment are disregarded when counting adults.

Find out more →

Council Tax Support

Income-related support for people on a low income. Separate from statutory discounts.
